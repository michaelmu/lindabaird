Linda Baird

Mezzo Soprano

About Linda

Praised for resonant, flexible singing, Canadian mezzo-soprano Linda Baird has sung in numerous opera companies and symphonies including: Seattle Opera, Vancouver Opera, Vancouver Symphony Orchestra, Northern Czech Philharmonic, San Jose Opera, Prince George Symphony, and Burnaby Lyric Opera.

Her 2017/2018 season includes her debut as Suzuki in Townsend Opera's Madame Butterfly, Carmen in California Opera Association's Carmen, Kate with San Luis Obispo Opera’s Madame Butterfly and Suzuki cover; Fricka with Heroic Opera’s Die Walküre in Vancouver, Canada; and Tisbe in California Opera’s La Cenerentola.

Other roles Linda has performed include: Dorabella Cosi fan tutte, Romeo I Capuleti ei Montecchi, Nora Lillian Alling, Rosina Barber of Seville, Charlotte Werther, Foreign Princess Rusalka, Iolanthe Iolanthe, Second Lady The Magic Flute, Valencienne The Merry Widow, The Grand Duchess The Goldoliers, Mercedes Carmen, Anna Les Troyen.

Linda is a native of North Vancouver, BC, Canada, and a Master of Music in Opera graduate from The University of British Columbia. Linda resides in San Francisco and works as a teaching artist for San Francisco Opera.
